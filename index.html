Research

My work combines fundamental electrochemistry with applied engineering — bridging academic depth and real-world impact.

Microkinetic analysis of anodic oxidation

Modeling reaction pathways and identifying rate-limiting steps in electrochemical transformations, including methoxylation of hydrocarbons.

  • Mechanistic interpretation from kinetics + experimental trends
  • Parameter sensitivity and performance optimization
  • Applications to electrosynthesis and Power-to-X

Organic electrosynthesis & sustainable chemistry

Developing electrochemical routes for cleaner chemical production using controllable electrode-driven reactions.

  • Electrode processes and operating conditions
  • Reaction selectivity and efficiency thinking
  • Scalability mindset for applied systems

Operando/analytical insight (Raman & data analysis)

Using spectroscopy and structured data analysis to connect measured signals with mechanism and stability.

  • Raman spectroscopy for reaction monitoring (as applicable)
  • Clean documentation and reproducible analysis
  • Strong emphasis on scientific communication

Selected Projects

A few projects across research and applied environmental engineering.

Master’s Thesis — PFAS/PFCs groundwater treatment

Atmospheric plasma technology for PFAS/PFCs-contaminated groundwater (Fraunhofer IGB, Stuttgart).

  • Optimized electrical + chemical parameters for high degradation
  • Investigated byproducts using ¹H-NMR
  • Tools: MS Office, MestReNova

YIP-Aqua-I — Wetland & filtration concept

Proposed an initial wetland + filtration design using locally available resources (UNIVA Mexico & SRH Heidelberg).

  • Field visits to farms, rivers, and Lake Chapala
  • Solid waste management + water habitat balance
  • Frugal innovation and humanitarian engineering framing

Microplastics degradation via AOPs

Compared UV/H₂O₂, Ozone/H₂O₂, and non-thermal plasma for LDPE microplastic degradation.

  • Comparative evaluation of AOP pathways
  • Poster presented at World Water Day (2022)
  • Clear reporting + presentation experience
